## STORY BEAT
**"You arrive. The world is ordinary. Until it isn't."**

The visitor lands on a bright, almost clean page. Deceptively calm. Five video clip containers in hexagonal/polygon shapes show fragments of Jin-Woo's world. The headline `MONARCH` is massive. The sub-copy is two lines that flip the visitor's world: *"You are the weakest hunter. Until the system chose you."*

As they begin to scroll, the clip containers collapse, the background darkens. The descent begins.
