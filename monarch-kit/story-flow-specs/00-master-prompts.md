## 🎬 How-To (One-Time Setup)
1. **Download reference images** from Google Images (search terms below).
2. **Upload each reference into Google Whisk** as the *Subject* image.
3. **Paste the prompt** (provided below) into Whisk.
4. **Whisk outputs** two image variants. Pick the best one.
5. **Download the chosen image** → this becomes your Start Frame or End Frame for Flow.
6. **Repeat for matching Start + End pair**.
7. **Upload both frames into Google Flow** → set tool to **Frames → Video**.
8. **Paste the Video Motion Prompt** (provided below). Generate.
9. **Download video. Done.**
