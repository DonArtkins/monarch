## CINEMATIC VOCABULARY — Rules for ALL Assets

### Color Language
| Token | Hex | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **Ice Blue** | `#60A5FA` | Jin-Woo's power, system gift, hope in darkness |
| **Deep Purple** | `#6D28D9` | Shadow energy, extraction, system truth |
| **Crimson** | `#FF2B2B` | Danger, S-rank gates, sacrifice |
| **Gold** | `#F5A623` | A-rank tier, divine authority |
| **True White** | `#F0F4FF` | The real world, before the system, vulnerability |
| **Void Black** | `#000000` | Absolute power, the shadow realm, transcendence |

### Lighting Rules
- **Light sections:** Harsh overhead fluorescent (real world, Hunter Association)
- **Portal transition:** Torch light + divine white light (ancient dungeon)
- **Dark sections:** Single directional key light from LEFT, deep shadows
- **Jin-Woo scenes:** Always rim-lit from behind with ice blue, face in shadow except eye glow
- **Monster/extraction scenes:** Red or purple underlit, fog at ground level

### Camera Rules
| Section | Camera | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Hero** | Wide, slight upward tilt | Jin-Woo is bigger than the world |
| **Origin** | Eye-level, tight | He is small here, not yet the Monarch |
| **Extraction** | Low angle, looking UP | He commands from above |
| **Monarchs** | Wide | They are incomprehensibly large |
| **Weapons** | Close-up, dramatic | Product shot, rotation implied |

## CHARACTER MASTER REFERENCE

> Copy the relevant row into EVERY prompt where that character appears. Do not rely on shorthand. The AI must see the full description.

### Sung Jin-Woo
```
Korean male, early-to-mid 20s. Lean, athletic build — not bulky, but clearly 
powerful. Sharp, angular jawline, slightly messy black hair (natural, not styled).

PRE-SYSTEM (Origin / E-Rank):
- Cheap padded hunter jacket, basic equipment, no armor
- BOTH eyes normal dark brown, no glow
- Expression: uncertain, tired, overlooked
- Body language: slightly hunched, deferential

POST-SYSTEM / MONARCH STATE (all dark sections):
- Matte black tactical longcoat, dark armored chest plate, black trousers, dark boots
- Left eye: cold electric ice blue glow, no pupil visible, like a light source
- Right eye: normal but intense
- Expression: calm, cold, absolute — he has decided everything already
- Body language: completely still, weight forward, zero wasted movement
- In battle: coat billows, shadow energy swirls like living smoke

NEVER: Anime proportions, unrealistic muscle, colored hair, cartoon stylization, 
CGI smoothness. ALWAYS: Real Korean actor, photorealistic Hollywood VFX quality.
```

### Igris (Shadow Knight General)
```
2.5 meters tall. Full dark plate armor — matte black, heavily segmented like a 
knight from a brutal age. Crowned helmet with a single vertical slit revealing 
two glowing red eyes. Carries an enormous black longsword (1.8 m long, matching 
his height). Shadow smoke trails from his joints — he is shadow given form.
Loyal to Jin-Woo — statue-still, awaiting command. Edges of armor slightly dissolve.
```

### Beru (The Ant King)
```
Giant insectoid creature, 3 meters on two legs. Chitinous black armor-like exoskeleton. 
Multiple arms. POST-EXTRACTION: Same form but shadow black, eyes glow blue. 
One knee on ground when acknowledging Jin-Woo. Surprisingly regal in stance.
```

### Baran (Ice Elf King — REIMAGINED as Shadow Soldier)
```
8-10 meters tall. Originally blue-white ice giant with elven features. 
POST-EXTRACTION (MONARCH version): Same form but dark — ice replaced by black frost, 
skin dark as shadow, crown of obsidian ice shards, eyes electric blue. 
Kneeling before Jin-Woo. The ground beneath him cracked with dark ice.
```

### The Architect
```
A humanoid figure 2.5 meters tall made entirely of pure white-gold radiant light. 
No face — just a brilliant oval of light. Geometric robes flow like living equations, 
mathematical fractals orbit the form. Radiates the uncanny — this is what made the system.
```

### Jin-Woo's Father (The Fallen Shadow Monarch)
```
A Korean man, late 40s, powerfully built, weathered face like an older Jin-Woo. 
Wearing ancient black armor — carved with age-runes. A dark crown. 
BOTH eyes glow deep blue, older, dimmer. Expression: weathered grief mixed with pride. 
Holds himself like a king who lost a war he was always going to lose.
```
