## 🎬 How-To (Individual Asset Generation)
1. **Choose an Asset** from the Table of Contents below.
2. **Download a reference image** from the internet (search terms provided in each section).
3. **Upload reference as the Subject** in Google Whisk.
4. **Paste the provided prompt** (it now contains ALL style and DNA info).
5. **For Videos**: Pick the best start/end frame. Download both.
6. **Upload both frames into Google Flow** set to **Frames → Video**.
7. **Paste the Video Motion Prompt**. Generate.
